Welcome, today we will learn how to make Sandwich

Discription

As we all know that sandwich is mostly used in morning breakfast

So today's recipes is all about Peanut Butter $ Banana Sandwhich

INGREDIENTS

  • Slice of Bread (toasted or plain )

  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter

  • 1 Banana (sliced)

  • Honey(optional)

Recipies

  1. Spread peanut butter on one side of each slice of bread
  2. Layer banana slices on top.
  3. Drizzle honey over the banana is desired.
  4. Close the sandwhich and enjoy

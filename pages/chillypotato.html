Welcome, today we will see how to make Chilli potato

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Cornflour
  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Oil
  • 3-4 cloves
  • Green chillies
  • Onion
  • Capsicum (bell pepper )
  • Soya sauce
  • Tomato Ketchup
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Spring Onions

Recipes for Make Chilli Potato

Prepare and Fry the Potatoes

  1. Boil the potato wedges for 3-4 minutes in water. Drain and pat dry with a paper towel
  2. In a bowl, mix the potatoes with cornflour , all-purpose flour a, pinch of salt, and a little wter to lightly coat the potatoes.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan and frythe coate potatoes until crispy and golden brown . Remove and palce on paper towels to drain excess oil.

Make the sauce

  1. heat 1-2 tablespoon of oil in pan. Add chopped garlic and green chillies and saute for a minute
  2. Add the onion and capscicum slices, and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes on meium heat
  3. Add soy sause , tomato ketchup , red chilli sause and a splash of vinegar . Stir and cook for a minute

Toss the Potatoes in the Sauce

  1. Add the fried potoes into the sauce and toss them well so they are evely coated
  2. Cook for another 1-2 minutes to combine all the flavors

Ingredients for Dosa Batter

  • Rice (regular or parboilded ) - 1.5 cups
  • Urad dal (split black gram lentils) – ½ cup
  • Fenugreek seeds (methi) – ½ teaspoon
  • water
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee

Steps to Make Dosa BAtter

  1. Soak the rice and Dal :
  2. Soak the rice and fengureek seeds together in a bowl, and the Urad dal in another bowl, for at least 4 to 6 hours
  3. Grind the Batter
  4. Ferment the Batter

Steps to Make Dosa

  1. Heath the pan
  2. Spread the Dosa : once the tawa is hot, pour a ladle of batter in the center and quickly spread it outwards in a circular motion using the back of the ladle to form a thin layer.
  3. Cook the Dosa
  4. Enjoy the dosa
